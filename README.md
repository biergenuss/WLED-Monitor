### Internal Wiring & Assembly
Inside, the ESP32 and the 2.0-inch SPI TFT display are modularly mounted. Wiring is cleanly routed, utilizing WAGO connectors for solid power distribution directly to the display backlight and logic rails.

* **Recommended Print Settings:**
  * Material: PLA or PETG (Carbon Fiber PLA recommended for the authentic look)
  * Layer Height: 0.2mm
  * Infill: 15% - 20%
  * Supports: Not required if oriented correctly
